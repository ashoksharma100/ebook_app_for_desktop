    Welcome to Pictionary

    A picture dictionary that helps you learn in a faster and an easier way

    Here you will see that all the words are listed in the alphabetical order (A-Z). When you click on a word under any alphabet, you will not just find its Meaning but also its Part of Speech, correct pronunciation, Synonyms, Antonyms and Picture. You will also learn how to use the word correctly in a sentence under usage. Use of animations for some words will help you learn faster. You can add words to your Favourites list to view later. You can also search for a word using the Search bar.

    • Click to listen to the audio of the pronunciation of the word.
    • Click to watch the animation of the word.
    • Click to change the theme.
    • Click (near ) to add the word to the list of Favourites.
    • Click (near the Search bar) to view the Favourites list.
    • Click to enlarge the related picture. To exit the full screen mode, click .
    • Click to record audio of a word and check its correct pronunciation.
    • Click to list down the recent words looked up.
    • Click to exit the Pictionary.
